# Safety Policy

This repository does not publish treatment guidance.

Do **not** use raw claim text as recommendation language.

- Do not treat transcript summaries as medical advice.
- Do not publish raw transcripts or long copyrighted excerpts.
- Route unsafe assertions (e.g., deprescribing, cancer/cardiovascular overclaiming)
  to `reviews/red-team/`.
- Any protocol claim for medicine/diabetes/cancer/kidney contexts must include
  clinician context and risk qualifiers.

## Public-safe baseline

- Claims are bounded by subgroup and context.
- Uncertainty and evidence limits are explicit.
- Risk classes and contraindications are visible in public exports.
