# Canonical Question

> **Which carnivore claims are true, for whom, under what conditions, with what risks, and with what level of evidence?**

## Interpretation

Every claim review must answer five dimensions:

| Dimension | Required question |
|---|---|
| Truth | Is the claim supported, contested, overstated, unsupported, contradicted, or not yet reviewed? |
| Population | Who does the claim apply to: general population, metabolically unhealthy, T2D, autoimmune, GI, psychiatric, athlete, kidney disease, pregnancy, pediatric, etc.? |
| Conditions | Under what diet context, duration, biomarkers, medications, disease state, food quality, monitoring, and behavior context? |
| Risks | What are the plausible harms, contraindications, failure modes, and clinical escalation triggers? |
| Evidence level | What is the evidence maturity: anecdote, case report, mechanism, observational, adjacent RCT, direct carnivore evidence, systematic review, guideline, or long-term outcome evidence? |

## Project stance

This ledger is not pro-carnivore and not anti-carnivore. It is a claim-first, provenance-aware, risk-gated, evidence-graded truth-seeking system.
